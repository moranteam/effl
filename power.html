If Everyone Played Everyone, Every Week

All Play Standings

What this means: your all play record is what would have happened if you had faced all seven opponents every single week, instead of the one the schedule happened to serve you. It strips the schedule luck out and measures pure scoring strength.

Actual Wins Minus Deserved Wins

The Luck Index

What this means: expected wins come from the all play table. If you actually won more games than your scoring deserved, your bar runs gold to the right (lucky). If the schedule robbed you, it runs maroon to the left (robbed). Bring this chart to the next argument.
