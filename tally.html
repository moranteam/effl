THE TALLY STATUTE · ARTICLE X

The owner whose franchise finishes in last place at the conclusion of a season shall receive a permanent tattoo of a tally mark, measuring no less than one inch. Placement is at the discretion of the finisher, with the buttock recognized as the traditional site. Tallies are cumulative over an owner's career and constitute the league's permanent standings of shame. Compliance is a matter of honor and public record.
